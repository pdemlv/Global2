Про компанію

Global Engineering Solutions is a leading supplier of parts and component units manufactured to individual customer drawings. With many years of experience and involvement of innovative equipment, we offer our clients the guaranteed quality, prompt delivery and convenient prices. Global Engineering Solutions is Polish subdivision of Swedish company “Claesson Engineering АВ” working in the metal industry for over 12 years. Successful track record is the key to the high-quality execution of orders of varying complexity. The Global Engineering Solutions Company is based in Poland and has its manufacturing facilities in the Eastern Europe. The technical potential of the company embodies the widest range of services in metalworking and manufacturing individual components for various areas of production. Our perfect location ensures the delivery of component units and parts in the most convenient for our clients manner.

services

turning services

If the customer requires to apply basic or additional coating, we engage our partners involved in galvanic and other types of coating. Thus, our clients just have to specify the desired type of coating in drawings, and we will provide it. After our partners perform all works, the parts undergo a multi-stage inspection on our site. In such a way, we appreciate the time and the trust our customers gave us and provide them with a full range of services. We have established a wide network of partners and metal suppliers, making the galvanic services and other processes much faster to perform and comply with the set requirements.
